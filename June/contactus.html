ABOUT MDA & UNIQUE ASSOCIATES


unique & MDA associates is a leading Nanded-based Construction company catering to world renowned clientele developing premium and luxury freehold properties in the Maharashtra.


The company has grown in stature, becoming a reputed and trusted property developer, boasting iconic clientele and prestigious projects in diversified fields, in the shortest time


From a meagre revenue of RS 200,000 in 2016, MDA & UNIQUE is expected to cross an accumulated revenue of RS 2 Million by 2022.


Mission & Vision


Established in 2002, Heilbronn has stayed true to its Mission of providing quality construction of buildings and roads and to offering job opportunities to hundreds of people.


Heilbronn aims to provide 500 more jobs in the next 2 years. The company’s Vision is to improve the welfare of its current 500 plus employees and construct more assets for rental yield.


With an unrivalled experience in sectors such as construction and property development, Heilbronn is a pioneer in buildings, roads and infrastructure, contracting and free hold property developments, among others.


Some of the prestigious projects undertaken by Heilbronn include Luxury Villas in Emirates Hills; Jumeirah Golf Estates; Rugby 7 Stadium for Emirates Airlines; Emirates Airlines Headquarters Building Roads; Emirates Aviation University; Emirates Flight Training Academy Runway; Emirates Flight Training Academy Simulator Building; Infrastructure jobs at the Mall of the Emirates and Madinat Jumeirah; Dnata Warehouses Roads; JW Marriott Marquis Hotel Roads & Parking; Emirates Sky Cargo Facilities at Al Maktoum International Airport; Emirates Flight Training Academy at Al Maktoum International Airport; Airbus A380 Hangers; Earthworks at Dubai International Airport; Al Maha Resort Roads; Emirates Cricket Ground and Pavilion, among numerous others.


Founded by Dileep Kumar, a civil engineer by profession, who leads from the front, the company ardently believes in lending a helping hand to uplift the needy. The CSR activities include providing financial assistance for underprivileged students and medical support to homeless adults. The company also offers jobs to engineering students, from low-income families, upon their completion of education.


Emirates Airlines, Dnata, City Properties, His Highness Sheikh Ahmed Bin Saeed Al Maktoum’s Real Estate Office, Al Bwardy Engineering and several other HNW private clientele vouch for Heilbronn’s professionalism and expertise.
